Odoo Website Guest Checkout

Make guest checkout available in Odoo!!

Odoo Website Guest Checkout Module allows you to apply restrictions on guest checkout without login / sign up, which is by default supported on odoo website. Admin can restrict guests to checkout without login / sign up, this way guest record could be maintained in a better way.

Restrict the guest checkout while placing order in Odoo!!

It is possible that they purchased a product as a guest and left the website without any assurance of returning back in the future. Being a store owner, it is important to retain your customers on your website.
The Odoo website guest checkout module basically restricts the guest checkout without login or sign up at the time of placing an order. On the checkout page, guest users will be asked to sign up or one step login to complete the purchase.
Fetching the details of a new customer can also help you to notify them about any offers and discounts going on your website. This will help you in increasing customer engagement and even your sales and profits.
